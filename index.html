Automating Trash Day Rescue

Finished 13.09.2023 for Github opensource

Ever forgot to take out the trash, only to face the wrath of a spouse? Been there, done that. But fear not! With a pinch of Python and a dash of automation, I've banished trash day forgetfulness. Picture this: me, a Python newbie and automation enthusiast, crafting a script to tackle one of life's great conundrums: the trash bin dilemma. I enlisted Selenium, the web automation wizard, to do my bidding. The code fires up the browser, logs into Berlin's trash collection portal, and hits the magical "Show all" button, unveiling the trash pickup schedule. But wait, there's more! It even clicks the "ICAL" button to export a calendar, ensuring I never miss trash day again. No code ZAPIER has the role to double-check, but ZAPIER is for kids. Now, my spouse needn't worry about the trash, and I'm the household hero (at least until the next chore). Thanks to tech, trash day squabbles are a thing of the past. And thus, automation prevails, one household chore at a time.

Empowering LIONS Club with a Digital Hub

Finished 14.08.2023 for Client

I had the privilege in 2007 to be president of LIONS Club, and now again as a passionate developer, bringing the club into the digital age that would streamline their operations and enhance their impact on the community. The website was carefully crafted to reflect the organization's mission and values while providing a user-friendly experience for both members and the public. Restricted Login Area: Understanding the need for secure access to club-related resources and discussions, I implemented a robust restricted login area. Club members could securely log in to gain access to a wealth of information, discussions, and documents reserved for their eyes only. This feature fostered a sense of community and collaboration among members while ensuring the confidentiality of sensitive club matters. Content Management System (CMS): To empower the club's administrators and streamline the management of social events, bureaucratic tasks, and financial matters, I integrated a Content Management System (CMS) into the website. This CMS allowed authorized users to effortlessly update and publish content, manage event calendars, share news, and handle financial records—all from a centralized and user-friendly dashboard. Enhancing Efficiency and Impact: The website's development and the inclusion of the login area and CMS had a profound impact on the LIONS Club's operations. Club members found it easier to access and share information, collaborate on projects, and stay updated on club activities. Administrators could efficiently manage the club's affairs, from scheduling events to handling finances, all within a secure online environment. Building a Stronger Community: Above all, the website helped foster a sense of unity and purpose among LIONS Club members. It became the go-to platform for communication, organization, and collaboration. The club's ability to connect with the community and carry out their philanthropic endeavors was greatly enhanced through this digital transformation. In the end, the LIONS Club website wasn't just about technology; it was about leveraging technology to strengthen an organization's ability to make a positive impact on society. It serves as a testament to the power of digital solutions in facilitating meaningful change.
